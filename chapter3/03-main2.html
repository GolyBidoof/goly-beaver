The analogy I like to use is being a jigsaw puzzle piece that you can clearly see has a rectangular blank.

Of course, you _want_ to fit, to belong, and so on, so you cram in as much play-doh as you can in order to be able to connect to other people's tabs.

When you're not actively looking for your needs being met, it's okay to just cram as much play-doh as you can.
To love is to change, right? So just let yourself change a little more, eventually someone will find the right tab for you.

Especially in friends terms, there's someone for everyone!!! Isn't that cool?

But as your expectations of yourself and that friend keep growing, how do you make sure that person is providing the right level of connection to you?
